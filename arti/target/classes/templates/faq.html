Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Monday to Sunday.

How can I book tickets?

You can book tickets online through our website or at the theater box office.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund for cancellations made at least 24 hours before the showtime.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 10 or more. Please contact us for more details.

Can I change my booking?

Yes, you can change your booking up to 2 hours before the showtime. Please contact us to make changes.
